Life Of Emmy

Emmylou Harper July Grace Edmiston, First of her Name, Born of Sir Luke of Blackbelly and Miss Golden Zoey of Idaho, Defier of State Lines, Crosser of Mountain Ranges, Protector of the Realm, Khalessi of the Poverty Bay, Chewer of Shoes, Heir to Savannah Queen of the Dog Park and Swimmer of the Sound, Chaser of Balls, Crier of the Kennel, and the Grower of Teeth.
